About

I began my venture into tech in the midst of the pandemic, realizing that the more skills I had that could be applied remotely, the better. At first, I approached it with a sense of practicality, knowing coding skills were in high demand — but that mindset changed quickly as I began to realize I had found something that I really loved to do. After many coding courses and hundreds of hours of YouTube tutorials, I decided to pursue web development full-time.

I use my love for design and eye for detail to write clean, organized code and create beautiful, functional developments — always with effective communication in mind.

I am passionate about making the world a more beautiful place.

I am a New Orleans girl, born and raised, but am trying out life in the Pacific North West for the moment. In my free time, I love to paint, journal, cook, or call my mom. You can find me on Sundays at the Ballard Farmer's Market shopping for local, fresh goodies.

Punk API Beer App

Using the Punk API, which is a database of beers, I was about to pull in that database, filter the beers by alc volume, by hoppyness, and added pagination to display the results.
